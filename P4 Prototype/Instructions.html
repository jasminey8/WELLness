Instructions

 

 

 

1. Log in with your Google account on the home page to provide your Google Calendar data.

2. Fill out each form in the Targets page to customize your wellness break. The number you enter in the “Sleep” form on the Targets page should be your ideal sleep goal. Once all selections are made, click on the “Save” button to save your information. Then, click on the Calendar icon to go to your new Google Calendar page with your wellness breaks added in.

3. Every day, you can click on an emoticon on the Check-Ins page that reflects your mood for the day. The mood you select will be updated in the “Your Mood Ratios” graph underneath the emoticons, so you can reflect on your mood history.

4. The Notifications page provides a history of your notifications. You will receive push notifications for wellness breaks.

5. The Settings page allows you to change your Google account, adjust the text size, and enable or disable notifications.
